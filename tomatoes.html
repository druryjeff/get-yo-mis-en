Mascerated Tomatoes

Published December 1, 2017

In this prep tip, we infuse fresh tomatoes with olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic and fresh basil. Not only do the tomatoes take on these flavors, they also release liquid that is ideal for creating a cooking liquid or sauce. The result can be used as a standalone salad, the base for a fresh pasta dish or a topping for bruscetta.

Ingredients

  • Fresh plum tomatoes, quartered
  • Olive oil
  • Course salt
  • Fresh ground pepper
  • Fresh basil, chopped
  • Fresh garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Assemble all ingredients in a mixing bowl and toss to coat.
  2. Let the mixture sit for a while (30 minutes or more). Toss occasionally.
  3. Serve as is a standalone salad or a topping for bruscetta. Add to a pan of softened onions to create the base for a fresh pasta dish. Here, the olive oil, garlic and tomato juices come together to form a flavorful coating for pasta or white beans (to name only two options).

Tips

  • The longer you let this sit, the more the flavor develops.
